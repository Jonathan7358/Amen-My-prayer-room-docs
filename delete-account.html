Request Account & Data Deletion

We value your privacy. If you wish to delete your Amen My Prayer Room account and remove all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Warning: Once your account is deleted, your prayer requests, testimonies, and profile information will be permanently removed. This action cannot be undone.

How to delete your account:

Option 1 (Inside the App): Go to Settings > Delete Account. This is the fastest way to remove your data immediately.

Option 2 (Web Request): If you have already uninstalled the app, you can send an email to our support team from your registered email address.

Email Support to Delete Account

Please include your full name and registered email address in the email. We will process your request and delete all data within 7 business days.
